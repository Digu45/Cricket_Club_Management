About the Cricket Management Club

Since 1995

Former Western Railways player and employee, Coach Dinesh Lad has been giving cricket Blaze Cricket coaching in Mumbai since 1995. Coach Lad has produced cricket stars such as Rohit Sharma, India's current captain, Shardul Thakur. His own son Siddhesh Lad is a Ranji Trophy winner. He has molded nearly 80.


Blaze is ranked #3 in the top 10 cricket clubs in India.Dinesh Lad started Blaze Cricket academy. To expand the business, he registered the firm as a public limited company. Membership fees and rental incomes from facilities owned/leased by the company generated the main revenues of the firm. The academy is doing great as some of the good players trained in the academy are representing their states and zonal teams in reputed domestic cricket leagues.

Our Mission Is To:
  • Foster a love for cricket among all ages and levels of skill.
  • Provide opportunities for individuals to play, coach, and organize cricket matches and tournaments.
  • Promote sportsmanship, teamwork, and fair play.
  • Support the development of cricket infrastructure and facilities.
  • Contribute to the growth and popularity of cricket worldwide.

CMC offers a wide range of services and programs, including:
  • Player registration and management.
  • Match scheduling and organization.
  • Tournament planning and execution.
  • Coaching and training programs.
  • Umpiring and scoring services.
  • Cricket equipment and apparel.
  • Cricket news and information.

CMC is committed to providing high-quality services and programs to our members and the wider cricket community. We are passionate about cricket and are dedicated to helping the sport thrive.
